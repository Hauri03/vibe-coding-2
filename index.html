About Our Mission

Making a Difference Together

Lions Club International is the world's largest service club organization, dedicated to serving communities worldwide. Our ice cream charity event brings our community together for a sweet cause.

Every purchase directly supports local families, educational programs, and community development projects. Your participation helps us continue our mission of serving those in need.
